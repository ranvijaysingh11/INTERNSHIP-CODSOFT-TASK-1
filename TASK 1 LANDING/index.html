Momos

Hailing from the Himalayan regions, momos are steamed dumplings with a delectable filling of minced meat or vegetables, wrapped in a thin dough. Served with spicy dipping sauces, momos have gained popularity worldwide for their unique flavors and comforting taste.

Maggie

Maggie is a brand of instant noodles loved globally for its quick preparation and delightful taste. It is a go-to comfort food, especially among college students and busy individuals, offering various flavors and the convenience of a satisfying meal in minutes.

Samosa

A popular Indian snack, samosa is a deep-fried pastry filled with a delicious mixture of spiced potatoes, peas, and sometimes minced meat. It is a crispy and savory treat enjoyed with chutneys and a favorite at tea time and street food stalls.
